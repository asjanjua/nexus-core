# Nexus Core

## Executive Intelligence for Teams That Need Clarity

Nexus Core turns documents, notes, and communication streams into evidence-backed briefs, dashboards, decisions, recommendations, and reusable company memory.

It is built for leaders who need to understand what matters faster, choose one workflow worth improving, and prove value with governed evidence instead of generic AI chat.

## What It Does
- Ingests enterprise content from docs and comms sources.
- Extracts risks, opportunities, tasks, decisions, and KPIs.
- Captures reusable notes in a Knowledge Workspace with backlinks, graph view, and portable Markdown.
- Maps findings into a lightweight ontology.
- Produces role-aware outputs for CEO, COO, and CBO / Strategy.
- Preserves source evidence so every insight can be traced.

## Why It Is Different
Most AI tools generate text.
Nexus is designed to generate usable executive context.

That means:
- evidence first
- role-specific outputs
- human approval for consequential actions
- readiness-first buyer routing
- one governed workflow pilot before broad rollout

## Try It
```bash
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/asjanjua/nexus-core/main/scripts/install.sh | bash
export PATH="$HOME/.nexus/bin:$PATH"
nexus init
nexus doctor
```

## Best For
- CEOs who want faster situational awareness
- COOs who need cleaner execution visibility
- Strategy leads who need evidence-backed recommendations
- Transformation teams running paid pilots
- Regulated or institution-facing teams that need approval gates and source trails

## Pilot Path

Nexus starts with readiness, routes the buyer into the right lane, selects one workflow pilot, and measures governed value proof through evidence refs, human review, and shadow ROI.
