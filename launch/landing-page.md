# Nexus Core

## Executive Intelligence for Teams That Need Clarity

Nexus Core turns documents and communication streams into evidence-backed briefs, dashboards, decisions, and recommendations.

It is built for leaders who need to understand what matters faster, with less manual synthesis and better provenance than generic AI chat.

## What It Does
- Ingests enterprise content from docs and comms sources.
- Extracts risks, opportunities, tasks, decisions, and KPIs.
- Maps findings into a lightweight ontology.
- Produces role-aware outputs for CEO, COO, and CBO / Strategy.
- Preserves source evidence so every insight can be traced.

## Why It Is Different
Most AI tools generate text.
Nexus is designed to generate usable executive context.

That means:
- evidence first
- role-specific outputs
- human approval for consequential actions
- a clean install path for pilots

## Try It
```bash
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/asjanjua/nexus-core/main/scripts/install.sh | bash
export PATH="$HOME/.nexus/bin:$PATH"
nexus init
nexus doctor
```
