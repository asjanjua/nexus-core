# Weekly Executive Brief

## Executive Summary
The current signal suggests the organization should prioritize operational clarity, decision ownership, and faster risk review.

## Top Risks
1. Decision bottlenecks are slowing execution.
2. Source content is fragmented across channels and folders.
3. Low-confidence inputs are still able to shape downstream work if not gated.

## Recommendations
1. Assign explicit owners for open decisions.
2. Tighten source provenance checks before executive distribution.
3. Review the most recent recommendations in a weekly approval queue.
