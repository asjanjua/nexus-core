3.1 Open Banking Framework (Phase 2)
The Central Bank published a consultation paper in April 2026 on Phase 2 of
the open banking framework, which will require licensed banks and EMIs to
expose account information APIs to accredited TPPs by Q1 2027. Our preliminary
assessment indicates a 6–9 month implementation effort. Budget request to be
submitted in the Q3 planning cycle.

3.2 Digital Asset Regulation
Draft regulations covering digital asset custody and stablecoin issuance were
circulated for industry comment in May 2026. Deadline for submissions: July 1.
Compliance is preparing our response. Current business model is not directly
affected, but the framework may create licensing requirements for our planned
crypto integration (roadmap item Q4 2027).

3.3 Enhanced Consumer Protection Rules
Effective January 1, 2027, the regulator will require banks and payment
providers to display total cost of credit (TCC) on all loan and BNPL products
at point-of-sale. Product team has been notified. Implementation estimated at
90 days once final rules are published (expected September 2026).
